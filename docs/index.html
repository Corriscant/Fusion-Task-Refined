Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 November 2025

This privacy policy describes how the mobile application Evolutionary Construct ("the App") handles user data.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and process device identifiers, approximate location, and usage data to provide and improve advertising. Data processing is governed by Google’s own privacy policy.

Data we collect

The developer does not collect or store personal data on their own servers. All data for ads and analytics is processed by third-party providers such as Google AdMob.

Children

The App is not directed to children under the age defined by local law for personalized advertising. Where required, only non-personalized ads are shown.
